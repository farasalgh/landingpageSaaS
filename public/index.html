Frequently Asked Questions

Got Questions?
We've Got Answers.

Find comprehensive information regarding orders, shipping, returns, product sizing, and other questions about your shopping experience.

What payment methods are available?

We accept credit/debit cards, bank transfers, PayPal, Stripe.

How long will it take for my order to be delivered?

Delivery times vary by location. Standard estimated delivery is 4 days. Please see our Shipping Policy page for more details.

Can I return or exchange a product?

Yes, we offer returns/exchanges within 7 of purchase. Please review our Returns & Exchanges Policy for full details

How can I track my order?

Once your order is shipped, you will receive an email with a tracking number. You can track your order directly on trackepacket.com.

How do I care for my garments to make them last?

Care instructions are provided on the label of each product. You can also find general garment care tips here caretips.com.

Are there any discounts for first-time purchases?

Yes, Sign up for our newsletter to get 10% off your first order!

How do I find my correct size?

We provide a detailed size guide correctsize.com on each product page to help you find your perfect fit.
